## Summary

The episode featured physician Eric Topol breaking down the science behind aging well, examining key factors like peptides, protein intake, sleep hygiene, and vaccines. Rather than accepting genetics as destiny, Topol addressed both legitimate longevity strategies and common myths, offering evidence-based insights into what actually determines how people age.

Listeners can focus on three concrete, actionable interventions: prioritize sleep hygiene, ensure adequate protein consumption, maintain vaccination status, and explore peptides if relevant to their health situation. The episode's core message is that aging outcomes aren't predetermined by genes—significant control exists through deliberate lifestyle and medical choices that measurably impact healthspan and lifespan.
