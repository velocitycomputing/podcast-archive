## Summary

Dr. Jason Hoffmann, an interventional radiologist at NYU Langone Health, discussed PAE best practices and program development strategies with host Dr. Michael Barraza. Key topics included building expertise within practices through coordination with urology departments, patient selection and expectation management, imaging technique optimization, and equipment choices. The episode covered the SwiftNinja Study findings, evolution of PAE guidelines (including the 2023 AUA BPH guidelines), insurance reimbursement challenges, and the importance of longitudinal care follow-up in interventional radiology. Discussion also addressed post-procedure patient satisfaction and management protocols.

Actionable steps for implementing a PAE program include: establishing referral relationships with urologists through coordinated patient workup; consulting the 2023 AUA Guidelines on Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia for evidence-based protocols; adopting steerable microcatheter techniques during superselective angiography to reduce radiation exposure and improve procedural efficiency; designing post-procedure care pathways to optimize patient satisfaction; and building systems for longitudinal follow-up care. Addressing insurance barriers upfront during program planning and investing in staff training on patient expectation-setting will support program sustainability.
