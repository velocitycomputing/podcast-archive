## Summary

This episode features interventional radiologist Dr. Preston Smith from Summit IR discussing prostate artery embolization (PAE), a minimally invasive procedure that treats benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) by shrinking an enlarged prostate through cutting off its blood supply. The discussion covers PAE as a mostly permanent treatment option and mentions that the underlying embolization technique—blocking arteries—is also used to address other conditions including uterine fibroids and arthritic knees.
