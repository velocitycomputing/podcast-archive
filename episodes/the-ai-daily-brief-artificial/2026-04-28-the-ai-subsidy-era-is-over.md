## Summary

The episode discusses how heavily-subsidized AI pricing is ending as agentic AI systems dramatically increase token consumption, forcing major providers like GitHub and Anthropic to shift toward usage-based billing models. NLW analyzes the implications of this pricing transition on technology markets, job displacement, and compute resource allocation across the industry.

Enterprises can reduce agent costs through five concrete moves: switching to cheaper models where feasible, conducting cost audits to understand where spending occurs, running model bake-offs to compare cost-to-performance ratios, building escape-hatch architectures that allow switching between approaches, and implementing transparent AI cost scorecards to track spending in real time. The podcast also references detailed guidance available at the linked play.aidailybrief.ai episode page.
